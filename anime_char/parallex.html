Favourite Anime Characters

Itachi Uchiha

Anime : Naruto

Itachi Uchiha was one of the supporting characters in the Naruto franchise. He was an ANBU Captain and a prodigy from Konohagakure's famed Uchiha clan. He had joined the criminal organization Akatsuki to serve as a double-spy, to later become an S-rank missing-ninja as he participated in murder of his entire clan on orders from higher-ups, in what would become known as the Uchiha clan massacre, defecting from the village subsequently.

" Those who cannot acknowledge themselves, will eventually fail. "

Levi Ackerman

Anime : Attack on Titans (AOT)

Levi Ackerman is the tritagonist of the anime/manga series Attack on titans. He is a Captain in the Survey Corps, known to be the strongest soldier alive. Levi is known to be a "clean-freak" man, as he tends to want stuff to be spotlessly clean. Moreover, he is often blunt and unapproachable and he has a strong respect for structure and discipline. My favourite scene of his was fight with Beast Titan.

" Give up on your dreams and die ! "

Kakashi Hatake

Anime : Naruto

Kakashi Hatake is the tetartagonist of the Naruto franchise and the leader of Team 7 later known as Team Kakashi. He is one of the strongest shinobi in the Hidden Leaf Village and can use a wide variety of Jutsu. Such Jutsus that he has are Lightning Blade, Kamui, Fire Style Jutsu which he gains due to his Sharingan and many others. After Kaguya's defeat, the Fifth Hokage Tsunade retired and Kakashi succeeded her as the Sixth Hokage before Naruto.

" Those who break the rules are scum, but those who abandon their friends are worse than scum "

Senku Ishigami

Anime : Dr. Stone

Senku Ishigami it the main protagonist of the Dr. Stone manga and anime series. He is friends with Taiju Oki and Yuzuriha Ogawa. He is a bright young scientist who strives to rebuild human civilization 3,700 years after the human race suddenly turned to stone. Senku is knowledgeable in many forms of Science. Despite is blunt nature, gaining many allies by impressing them with his scientific knowledge. Senku is very weak, According to Kohaku, his level of strength would be 3. He can't walk for too long or else he gets exhausted.

“ There's nothing you can't do if you try! ”

Kuro Totsuro

Anime : Haikyuu

Kuroo Tetsuro was previously a third-year student from Nekoma High. He was the Boys' Volleyball Club's captain and a middle blocker, known as the "Scheming Captain". As of November 2018, he is part of the Japan Volleyball Association's sports promotion division.

" Remember. We are blood. Never stop flowing. Keep moving. Keep bringing oxygen...So that our "brain"...can operate at his best. "

Koro Sensei

Anime : Assassination Classroom

Korosensei was the homeroom teacher for Class 3-E of Kunugigaoka Junior High School, and the deuteragonist of Assassination Classroom. He claimed to be responsible for creating the ermanent crescent moon and said that he planned to destroy the earth after "teaching" Class 3-E for a year.

" The difference between the novice and the master is that the master has failed more times than the novice has tried. "

Giyu Tomioko

Anime : Demon Slayer

Giyu Tomioka is a Demon Slayer and the Water Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps. As a Pillar of the Demon Slayer Corps, Giyu is a very powerful swordsman. He is able to easily defeat the Father Spider Demon in his transformed state. Giyu is very fast, being able to tie up Inosuke Hashibira before he even realized what happened. He was also able to cut off Rui's head in the blink of an eye.

" Feel The Rage. The Powerful, Pure Rage Of Not Being Able To Forgive Will Become Your Unswerving Drive To Take Action. "
